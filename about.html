Below you can find more information about our company

Neighbours helping neighbours: Fresh Food Club is a disruptive farm to fork concept which aims to harvest the fresh food available from local gardens and fruit trees located on city owned land and local homeowners to provide to the local Food Bank and Homeless shelters in an effort to raise the qaulity of food distributed and help reduce poverty overall.

Our Mission

Through community partnerships, we obtain and distribute fresh food from our neighbours for our neighbours

Why Choose Us

Neighbours helping neighbours. Helping your neighbour in unexpected ways can be as simple and easy as sharing extra food that your garden produces or perhaps you have a fruit tree on your property - harvesting and providing it to the local food bank will help reduce food insecurity everywhere.
